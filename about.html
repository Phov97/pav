    How it all began

    The idea came out of no where one day. It was something I wanted to do to express myself, it then went further than that! This became the foundation of the what the Phovcast is slowly becoming, a platform to help people deal with their feelings. The range of guests we've had on has allowed us to share different experiences and open wider conversations. Come join the conversation and take a listen to why over a 1000 other people have listened!

    What do we talk about?

    What don't we talk about is the better question! We talk about the most meaningful things, for example: Sport, feelings, veganism, world politics, how we should handle situations and how to be happy
